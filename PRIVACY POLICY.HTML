PRIVACY POLICY


As determined by the General Data Protection Law (LGPD, Law No. 13,709/2018) and by the rules and regulations of ANATEL and ANCINE, EasyTV is
committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of its users and visitors.


1. Information Collected.


We collect personal data that you actively provide during registration, as well as data that is automatically collected when you visit our Landing Page
and use our APPs, such as, but not limited to, IP address, device information, cookies, and page usage patterns.


2. Purpose of Processing.


The data is processed to:


– Offer, enhance, and personalize our services and products;

– Comply with regulatory requirements of ANATEL and ANCINE and other competent authorities;

– Prevent fraud and improve security;

– Send you relevant and promotional communications.


3. Information Sharing.


We do not sell personal data. However, data may be shared with:


– Partners and suppliers, under strict confidentiality agreements;

– Regulatory entities and authorities, such as the Central Bank, when required by law or to comply with regulatory obligations.


4. Data Security.


We take administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal data from loss, theft, and misuse, as well as from unauthorized access,
disclosure, alteration, and destruction.


5. Rightholder's Rights.


Respecting the LGPD and the regulations of ANATEL and ANCINE, we guarantee the data subject:


– Confirmation of the existence of processing and access to data;

– Correction of incomplete, inaccurate or outdated data;

– Anonymization, blocking or deletion of unnecessary or non-compliant data;

– Portability of your data to another service or product, upon express request;

– Information about the public or private entities with which we share your data;

– Revocation of consent, being alerted to the consequences that may arise from the denial.


6. Retention of Information.


We keep your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including to comply with any legal, regulatory,
auditing requirements, and to preserve rights.


7. Changes to the Privacy Policy.


EasyTV reserves the right to update or modify this Policy from time to time in accordance with legislative changes or business needs.


8. Contact and Questions.


For any questions, requests or complaints related to privacy and data protection, please contact us via email


9. Consent.


By using our services and products, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
